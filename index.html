About Us

Empathy+Action=Change is a cooperative alliance built on empathy and kindness, focused on marginalized individuals to strengthen and empower them to actively take part in the growth of their community.

Where love and belonging thrive, this group encourages connection, engagement, acceptance, action, transformation, and learning. We believe that when we combine genuine empathy with meaningful action, we create lasting positive change in our communities.
